Introduction¶

The task is to automate the warehouse with the realm of IOT. This starts with keeping a check on the inventory to know the number and details of items and then publishing them to the inventory sheets. Then the orders are recieved through the MQTT subscription to which the the node node_action_server_ros_iot_bridge takes it and publishes them to the ROS topic /ros_iot_bridge/mqtt/sub. This topic is then accessed by another node node_pick_place_iot_client and then it is processed and given to diffrent action clients that are integrated with the ur5 arms that pick and place the orders and send results such as dispachted orders, shipped orders etc to push the data in the respective sheets. The approach to the problem was to first accces the packages present in the inventory and get their details such as their colors and storage position using image processing by the 2-D camera. This detail of the inventory is then send to the ROS IOT BRIGDE to process and push the data to the xcel sheets. This data is also passed to the various MoveIt commanders so that the pickup and ispatch of orders take place accordingly. The the actionserver upon the complettion of the goal sends resluts to their various action clients who then pass the data to the ros iot bridge to publish he data to the dashboards and xcel sheets.
